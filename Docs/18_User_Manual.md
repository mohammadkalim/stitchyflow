## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Welcome to StitchyFlow
StitchyFlow is a comprehensive platform that connects customers with professional tailors, making custom tailoring services accessible and convenient.

### 1.2 Who Can Use StitchyFlow?
- **Customers**: Find tailors and place custom orders
- **Tailors**: Manage orders and grow your business
- **Business Owners**: Manage tailoring business operations
- **Administrators**: Oversee the entire platform

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Creating an Account

1. Visit the StitchyFlow website
2. Click "Sign Up" button
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First name and last name
   - Phone number
   - Select your role (Customer/Tailor/Business Owner)
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Click the verification link
7. Wait for approval (for Tailor/Business Owner accounts)

### 2.2 Logging In

1. Visit the StitchyFlow website
2. Click "Login" button
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### 2.3 Forgot Password

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the reset link
5. Enter new password
6. Click "Reset Password"

## 3. Customer Guide

### 3.1 Browsing Tailors

1. Login to your account
2. Click "Browse Tailors" from menu
3. View list of available tailors
4. Use filters to narrow search:
   - Location
   - Rating
   - Specialization
   - Price range
5. Click on tailor card to view profile

### 3.2 Viewing Tailor Profile

Tailor profiles show:
- Profile photo
- Name and experience
- Specializations
- Ratings and reviews
- Completed orders
- Sample work photos
- Pricing information

### 3.3 Placing an Order

1. Select a tailor
2. Click "Place Order" button
3. Fill in order details:
   - **Garment Type**: Select from dropdown
   - **Measurements**: Enter your measurements
     - Chest
     - Waist
     - Hips
     - Shoulder width
     - Sleeve length
     - Other measurements
   - **Special Instructions**: Any specific requirements
   - **Reference Images**: Upload photos (optional)
4. Review order summary
5. Click "Submit Order"
6. Proceed to payment (if required)
7. Receive order confirmation

### 3.4 Tracking Orders

1. Go to "My Orders" from dashboard
2. View list of all your orders
3. Click on an order to see details:
   - Order number
   - Current status
   - Tailor information
   - Order specifications
   - Estimated completion date
   - Status history
4. Receive notifications for status updates

### 3.5 Order Status Meanings

- **Pending**: Order submitted, awaiting tailor acceptance
- **Accepted**: Tailor has accepted your order
- **In Progress**: Tailor is working on your order
- **Completed**: Order is ready for pickup/delivery
- **Cancelled**: Order has been cancelled

### 3.6 Communicating with Tailor

1. Open your order details
2. Click "Message Tailor" button
3. Type your message
4. Click "Send"
5. Receive notifications for replies

### 3.7 Submitting a Review

1. Go to completed order
2. Click "Write Review" button
3. Select rating (1-5 stars)
4. Write your review comment
5. Upload photos (optional)
6. Click "Submit Review"

## 4. Tailor Guide

### 4.1 Setting Up Your Profile

1. Login to your account
2. Go to "My Profile"
3. Complete your profile:
   - Upload profile photo
   - Add bio and description
   - List specializations
   - Add experience details
   - Upload portfolio images
   - Set pricing information
4. Click "Save Profile"

### 4.2 Managing Orders

#### Viewing New Orders
1. Check "Pending Orders" section
2. Click on order to view details
3. Review customer requirements
4. Check measurements and specifications

#### Accepting Orders
1. Open pending order
2. Review all details carefully
3. Click "Accept Order" button
4. Order moves to "Active Orders"
5. Customer receives notification

#### Rejecting Orders
1. Open pending order
2. Click "Reject Order" button
3. Provide reason for rejection
4. Click "Confirm Rejection"
5. Customer receives notification

### 4.3 Updating Order Status

1. Go to "Active Orders"
2. Select an order
3. Click "Update Status"
4. Select new status:
   - In Progress
   - Completed
5. Add progress notes (optional)
6. Upload progress photos (optional)
7. Click "Update"
8. Customer receives notification

### 4.4 Managing Availability

1. Go to "Settings"
2. Click "Availability"
3. Set your status:
   - Available
   - Busy
   - Unavailable
4. Set capacity (max orders)
5. Click "Save"

### 4.5 Viewing Earnings

1. Go to "Earnings" section
2. View total earnings
3. See payment history
4. Check pending payments
5. Download payment reports

## 6. Admin Guide

### 6.1 User Management

1. Login to admin panel
2. Go to "Users" section
3. View all users
4. Filter by role
5. Search for specific user
6. View user details
7. Edit user information
8. Suspend/activate accounts
9. Delete users (if necessary)

### 6.2 Approving Registrations

#### Business Owner Approval
1. Go to "Pending Approvals"
2. Click on business owner registration
3. Review submitted information
4. Verify business details
5. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
6. User receives email notification

#### Tailor Approval
1. Go to "Pending Approvals"
2. Click on tailor registration
3. Review profile and credentials
4. Verify experience and skills
5. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
6. User receives email notification

### 6.3 System Configuration

1. Go to "Settings"
2. Configure system parameters:
   - Commission rates
   - Email templates
   - Notification settings
   - Payment settings
3. Click "Save Configuration"

### 6.4 Viewing Analytics

1. Go to "Dashboard"
2. View platform statistics:
   - Total users
   - Total orders
   - Revenue
   - Growth metrics
3. Generate reports
4. Export data

## 8. Troubleshooting

### 8.1 Can't Login
- Verify email and password are correct
- Check if account is activated
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if issue persists

### 8.2 Order Not Showing
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Verify order was submitted successfully
- Contact support with order number

### 8.3 Payment Issues
- Verify payment information
- Check bank/card status
- Try different payment method
- Contact support for assistance

### 8.4 Upload Issues
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Verify file format (JPEG, PNG)
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection
