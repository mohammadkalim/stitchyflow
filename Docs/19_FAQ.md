## General Questions

### What is StitchyFlow?
StitchyFlow is a digital platform that connects customers with professional tailors for custom tailoring services. It provides an easy way to find tailors, place orders, track progress, and manage tailoring businesses.

### Who can use StitchyFlow?
- Customers looking for tailoring services
- Professional tailors wanting to grow their business
- Business owners managing tailoring operations
- Administrators overseeing the platform

### Is StitchyFlow free to use?
Registration is free for all users. Commission fees may apply to completed orders for business owners and tailors.

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
1. Visit the StitchyFlow website
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Fill in your details
4. Verify your email
5. Wait for approval (for Tailor/Business Owner accounts)

### Why is my account pending approval?
Tailor and Business Owner accounts require admin approval to ensure quality and authenticity. This usually takes 24-48 hours.

### I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, go to Settings > Profile and update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email.

### How do I delete my account?
Contact support at support@stitchyflow.com to request account deletion.

## For Customers

### How do I find a tailor?
1. Login to your account
2. Click "Browse Tailors"
3. Use filters to narrow your search
4. View tailor profiles and ratings
5. Select a tailor

### How do I place an order?
1. Select a tailor
2. Click "Place Order"
3. Fill in garment details and measurements
4. Add special instructions
5. Upload reference images (optional)
6. Submit order

### What measurements do I need?
Common measurements include:
- Chest
- Waist
- Hips
- Shoulder width
- Sleeve length
- Shirt/pant length
Specific requirements vary by garment type.

### How do I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" in your dashboard to see all orders and their current status.

### Can I cancel an order?
Yes, you can cancel orders before the tailor accepts them. Contact the tailor or support for accepted orders.

### How do I pay for orders?
Payment methods and timing depend on the tailor's preferences. Options may include cash, card, or online payment.

### Can I modify my order after submission?
Contact the tailor directly through the messaging system to discuss modifications.

### How do I leave a review?
After order completion, go to the order details and click "Write Review" to rate and review the tailor.

## For Tailors

### How do I become a tailor on StitchyFlow?
1. Register with role "Tailor"
2. Complete your profile with experience and skills
3. Upload portfolio images
4. Wait for admin approval
5. Start receiving orders

### How do I receive orders?
Orders appear in your "Pending Orders" section. You'll also receive email notifications.

### Can I reject an order?
Yes, you can reject orders with a reason. This helps maintain quality and manage your workload.

### How do I update order status?
Go to "Active Orders", select an order, click "Update Status", choose new status, and add notes if needed.

### How do I get paid?
Payment arrangements are made directly with customers or through the business owner you're associated with.

### Can I set my own prices?
Yes, you can set your pricing information in your profile settings.

### How do I manage my availability?
Go to Settings > Availability to set your status (Available/Busy/Unavailable) and maximum order capacity.

### What if I have a dispute with a customer?
Contact support immediately at support@stitchyflow.com for assistance with disputes.

---

## For Business Owners

### How do I add tailors to my business?
Go to "Tailors" section, click "Add Tailor", and either create a new tailor account or invite existing tailors.

### Can I manage multiple tailors?
Yes, you can manage multiple tailors and assign orders to them.

### How do I view business analytics?
Go to "Analytics" dashboard to view orders, revenue, tailor performance, and other metrics.

### Can I set commission rates?
Commission rates are set by the platform admin. Contact support for custom arrangements.

### How do I handle customer complaints?
Monitor orders closely, communicate with customers, and contact support if needed.

## Orders & Payments

### What is the order process?
1. Customer places order
2. Tailor receives notification
3. Tailor accepts/rejects order
4. Tailor works on order
5. Tailor updates status
6. Order completed
7. Customer reviews

### How long does an order take?
Completion time varies by garment type and tailor. Estimated completion dates are provided when placing orders.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Payment methods vary by tailor and may include cash, card, bank transfer, or online payment.

### Can I get a refund?
Refund policies depend on the tailor and order status. Contact the tailor or support for refund requests.

### Do I get an invoice?
Yes, invoices are generated for all completed orders and can be downloaded from your order history.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't login
- Verify email and password
- Check if account is activated
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support

### My order isn't showing
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Verify order submission
- Contact support with order number

### Images won't upload
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Verify file format (JPEG, PNG)
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection

### I'm not receiving notifications
- Check notification settings
- Verify email address
- Check spam folder
- Enable browser notifications
